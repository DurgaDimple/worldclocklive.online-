What is Daylight Saving Time? Daylight Saving Time (DST) is the practice of setting clocks forward by one hour during warmer months to extend evening daylight. Most countries in North America and Europe observe DST, while many countries in Asia and Africa do not.

2026 DST Schedule: In 2026, DST begins in spring (March for Northern Hemisphere, September/October for Southern Hemisphere) and ends in fall (October/November for Northern Hemisphere, March/April for Southern Hemisphere).
