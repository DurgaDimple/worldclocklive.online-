   - Turn blog posts into YouTube scripts
   - Convert city pages into social media posts

### SEO Quick Wins:

1. **Target Long-Tail First**
   - "time in {small city} {country}" = easier ranking
   - Build up to competitive terms

2. **Internal Linking is KEY**
   - Link every new page to 5-8 existing pages
   - Update old pages with links to new content

3. **Schema Markup = Featured Snippets**
   - Use templates provided (especially FAQ schema)
   - Google loves structured data

### Traffic Acceleration:

1. **Leverage Seasonal Peaks**

   - Sports events with global audiences

2. **Build Tools People Share**
   - Alarm clock is shareable
   - Meeting planner is viral
   - Countdown timers get links

3. **Create "Best of" Lists**
   - "Best cities for remote workers by timezone"
   - "Top 10 countries with weird time zones"
   - People love listicles
